Project 3 Proposal: Aim Trainer

Concept

A fast-paced click aim trainer where players must hit as many targets as possible in 30 seconds. Three targets are always on screen and when one is hit, it instantly reappears elsewhere. There is also a leaderboard to track all of their top 10 highest scores. This is a simple, addictive, and skill-based game.

Genre

  • Arcade
  • Aim Trainer (similar to FPS warm-up tools)
  • Clicker / Reaction

Aesthetics

  • Graphics: Clean, minimalist UI with gradients and shawdows.
  • Style: Dark theme with smooth animations and effects on hit.
  • Sound: Satisfying click/hit sounds for targets and UI.

Gameplay

Controls:

  • Mouse clicks to hit targets.

Mechanics:

  • 3 targets on screen at all times.
  • Targets reposition instantly when hit.
  • 30-second countdown timer.
  • Score increases by 10 per hit.
  • Game ends when timer reaches 00:00.
  • Final score displayed with option to restart/play again.
